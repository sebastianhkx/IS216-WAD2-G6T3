- In ConnectionManager.php, verify that the username, password, and port number are correct for your local
computer setup.
- Import load.sql into your local MySQL database (via PHPMyAdmin, WorkBench or by other means).

Step 2:
- Open the folder through your own localhost and you will be brought to a Login Form
- Click on the Register

Step 3:
- Click on the Register your BOT and follow the instructions
- Add the bot @scheduler34_bot
- Send /start to the bot

Step 4:
- Register by inputing your username and password and telegram Handle

Step 5:
- Once registration is done, you be redirected to the login Page.

Step 6: 
- Login with your registered username and password.

Step 7:
- Enjoy ScheduleLyfe!!
